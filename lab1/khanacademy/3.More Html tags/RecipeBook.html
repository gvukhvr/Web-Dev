Recipe #1 Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Milk 1 or 1/2 cups

Steps: Mix the flour, milk, eggs, and sugar in a large bowl until smooth. Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter for each pancake and cook until golden on both sides. Serve with syrup or fresh fruit.

Recipe #2 Creamy Alfredo Pasta

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 300g
Heavy Cream 1 cup

Steps: Cook the pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. In a saucepan, heat heavy cream and butter over low heat, then add Parmesan cheese. Combine with pasta and serve hot.
